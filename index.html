How we Started?

We will like to introduce CLIMIFI as a bridge between green fund seekers and green funders.


We saw that many businesses focused towards climate change are not properly funded or are facing their own set of challenges to establish their product. Currently, there is significant lack of awareness in the market for the green products.


As a matter of fact, currently only 3.6% of funding is towards environment friendly projects. This is something alarming since climate conservation is not an option now, it’s a need of an hour.


Till date, we have successfully worked with over 200+Sustainable founders and boost a network of over 500+ investors focused towards making impact investments
